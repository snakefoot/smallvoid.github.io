##### Description:

 Shares Windows Media Player libraries to other networked players and media devices using Universal Plug and Play.
 
##### Recommended state:

- Manual

##### Default State:

- Manual

##### Proces name:

- wmpnetwk.exe (WMPNetworkSvc)

##### Supports:

- none

##### Depends:

- [UPnP Device Host](/article/winnt-services-upnphost.html)
